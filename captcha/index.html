Captcha Errors


We use Google's reCaptcha service as a way to prevent bots and spammers from our site. For example, when signing up or submitting a form on this website, you may be asked to fill out a Captcha (hence, the "Verifying your Humanity" message that appears). Other information about Captchas and reCaptcha v3 can be found here.

School firewalls and insecure connections are the two most common errors that occur. First, try using different the different browsers on your computer. For example, while Internet Explorer may cause the Captcha to break, Google Chrome may not. If the errors continue to persist, you most likely either have a network firewall (or browser extension) blocking the Captcha or there is a generic connection error (no Internet).

If these errors are preventing you from creating an account, logging in, or submitting a suggestion, you can email me your problem, and we can work out a manual solution. In the meantime, thank you for your patience!
